Spaghetti Recipe

This recipe will help you get your fill of veggies! Itâ€™s a great way to eat the rainbow! You can use wheat or gluten-free pasta. We choose spaghetti but you could use your favorite shape.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 15 minutes
  • Preparation: 10 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Salt, Pepper
  • Mushrooms
  • Broccoli
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Prepare pasta: Dump the Zucchini, Broccoli, Carrots, Mushrooms, Onions, Tomatoes and Garlic into a hot pan. I prefer a skillet or Dutch Oven (something with a large base).
  2. Cook the Veggies: Cook the veggies for about 7 minutes. If the veggies begin to stick or burn, add a few tablespoons of water as often as needed. Cover with a lid, stir every few minutes.
  3. Cook for another 5 minutes: Cook for 5 minutes while the Spinach wilts. The veggies should be tender and the grape tomatoes will have burst.
  4. Add fillings (optional): When the pasta begin to set but are still slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette.
  5. Pour the drained water: Pour the drained spaghetti into the veggies and mix well. Add salt and pepper if needed.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
