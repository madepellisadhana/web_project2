PANCAKES

Ingredients:

1 cup all-purpose flour
2 tablespoons sugar
1 tablespoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 egg
1 cup milk
2 tablespoons melted butter or vegetable oil
1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)

Instructions:

In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. In another bowl, beat the egg and then stir in the milk, melted butter or oil, and vanilla extract (if using).
Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps are okay. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease it with butter or oil.
Pour about 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancake and the edges look set, about 2-3 minutes.
Flip the pancake and cook for another 1-2 minutes, until golden brown on both sides. Repeat with the remaining batter, greasing the skillet as needed.
Serve the pancakes warm with your favorite toppings such as maple syrup, fresh fruit, or whipped cream.

Enjoy your delicious homemade pancakes!

JUCIES

Ingredients:

Assorted fruits (e.g., oranges, apples, grapes, strawberries, pineapple, watermelon, etc.)
Water or ice cubes (optional)
Sweetener such as honey, sugar, or agave syrup (optional)

Instructions:

Wash all the fruits thoroughly under running water.
Peel and chop the fruits into small pieces, removing any seeds or pits. Place the chopped fruits into a blender or juicer. If desired, add a little water or ice cubes to help with blending and to adjust the consistency of the juice.
Blend the fruits until smooth. If using a juicer, follow the manufacturer's instructions. Taste the juice and add sweetener if desired. Blend again to combine.
Strain the juice through a fine mesh sieve or cheesecloth to remove any pulp or solids, if preferred. Transfer the juice to a pitcher or serving glasses.
Serve the juice immediately over ice, or refrigerate it for later. Stir the juice before serving, as natural separation may occur over time.

Enjoy your refreshing homemade fruit juice! You can customize the recipe by using your favorite fruits or by adding herbs like mint or basil for extra flavor. Adjust the sweetness according to your taste preferences.

SUGAR-COOKIE

Ingredients:

2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1 cup unsalted butter, softened
1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
1 large egg
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
Additional sugar for rolling (optional)
Instructions:

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper or silicone baking mats. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and baking powder. Set aside.
In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the egg and vanilla extract until well combined. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until a smooth dough forms.
If desired, roll rounded tablespoonfuls of dough into balls and then roll them in granulated sugar to coat. Place the dough balls onto the prepared baking sheets, spacing them about 2 inches apart. Use the bottom of a glass to gently flatten each dough ball until it's about 1/4 inch thick. Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for 8 to 10 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden.
Remove the cookies from the oven and let them cool on the baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring them to wire racks to cool completely. Once cooled, store the cookies in an airtight container at room temperature. Enjoy your homemade sugar cookies with a glass of milk or your favorite hot beverage!

Feel free to decorate these sugar cookies with frosting, sprinkles, or colored sugar for extra flair, or enjoy them plain for a simple treat.

CHICKEN STRIPS

Ingredients:

500g chicken breast fillets, cut into strips
1 cup all-purpose flour
2 eggs, beaten
1 cup breadcrumbs
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 teaspoon paprika
1 teaspoon garlic powder
Salt and pepper to taste
Cooking oil for frying

Instructions:

Preheat the Oven:
Preheat your oven to 200°C (400°F).
Prepare Breading Station:
Set up a breading station with three shallow dishes. Fill one with flour, one with beaten eggs, and one with a mixture of breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
Coat Chicken Strips:
Dredge each chicken strip in flour, shaking off any excess. Dip the floured chicken strip into the beaten eggs, allowing any excess to drip off.
Coat the chicken strip thoroughly with the breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to adhere the crumbs.
Bake the Chicken Strips:
Place the breaded chicken strips on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the coating is golden and crispy.

Serve:
Once cooked, remove the chicken strips from the oven and let them cool slightly. Serve the crispy chicken strips with your favorite dipping sauce, such as barbecue sauce, honey mustard, or ranch dressing.

Enjoy your homemade crispy chicken strips as a delicious snack or main dish!

CHOCOLATE CAKE

Ingredients:

2 cups all-purpose flour
1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
1 cup milk
1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
3 large eggs
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 teaspoon salt
Instructions:

Preheat the Oven:
Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
Mix Dry Ingredients:
In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt until well combined.
Combine Wet Ingredients:
In another bowl, beat the softened butter until creamy. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients:
Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the milk. Begin and end with the dry ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.
Bake the Cake:
Divide the batter evenly between the prepared cake pans. Smooth the tops with a spatula. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
Remove the cakes from the oven and allow them to cool in the pans for 10 minutes. Then, transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.
Frost and Decorate:
Once the cakes are completely cooled, you can frost and decorate them as desired. You can use buttercream frosting, whipped cream, or any other frosting of your choice. Add sprinkles, fresh fruit, or other decorations to personalize your cake.

Serve and Enjoy:
Slice the cake and serve it to your guests, family, or enjoy it yourself with a cup of tea or coffee. This classic vanilla sponge cake is perfect for birthdays, celebrations, or any time you're craving a delicious homemade treat!

CUPCAKES

Ingredients:

1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
1 cup granulated sugar
2 large eggs, at room temperature
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1/2 cup milk, at room temperature
Instructions:

Preheat the Oven:
Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.
Mix Dry Ingredients:
In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.
Cream Butter and Sugar:
In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy, using a hand mixer or stand mixer.
Add Eggs and Vanilla:
Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Then, mix in the vanilla extract until well combined.
Alternate Adding Dry Ingredients and Milk:
Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, alternating with the milk. Begin and end with the dry ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.
Fill Cupcake Liners:
Spoon the batter into the prepared cupcake liners, filling each about 2/3 full.
Bake the Cupcakes:
Bake in the preheated oven for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
Cool and Frost:
Add sprinkles, chocolate chips, or other decorations to the frosted cupcakes to personalize them and make them extra special.
Serve and Enjoy:

Arrange the cupcakes on a serving platter and enjoy them as a delicious treat for birthdays, parties, or any occasion!
These vanilla cupcakes are sure to be a hit with family and friends. Feel free to get creative with different frosting flavors and decorations to suit your taste preferences!

APPLE TOAST

Ingredients:

2 slices of bread (white, wheat, or any bread of your choice)
1 apple, thinly sliced
2 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
2 tablespoons brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
Pinch of nutmeg (optional)
Maple syrup or honey, for drizzling (optional)
Instructions:

Preheat the Oven:
Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
Prepare the Bread:
Place the bread slices on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper or aluminum foil.
Spread Butter:
Spread 1 tablespoon of softened butter evenly over each slice of bread.
Arrange Apple Slices:
Arrange the thinly sliced apple pieces evenly over the buttered bread slices, overlapping them slightly if needed.
Sprinkle Cinnamon and Sugar:
Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for about 10-12 minutes, or until the edges of the bread are golden brown and the apples are tender.
Drizzle with Syrup (optional): If desired, drizzle the apple cinnamon toast with maple syrup or honey just before serving for added sweetness and flavor.
Serve Warm:
Remove the toast from the oven and let it cool for a minute or two before serving. Serve warm and enjoy!
This apple cinnamon toast makes for a delicious breakfast or snack, with the warm, comforting flavors of cinnamon and brown sugar paired with the sweetness of the apples. It's perfect for cozy mornings or anytime you're craving a tasty treat!

CHURROS

Ingredients:

1 cup water
2 1/2 tablespoons white sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 cup all-purpose flour
2 quarts oil for frying
1/2 cup white sugar, or to taste
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
Instructions:

Preheat Oil:
Heat the 2 quarts of oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Make Dough:
In a small saucepan over medium heat, combine water, 2 1/2 tablespoons sugar, salt, and 2 tablespoons vegetable oil. Bring to a boil and remove from heat. Stir in flour until mixture forms a ball.
Fry Churros:
Scoop the dough into a piping bag fitted with a large star tip. Pipe strips of dough directly into the hot oil, cutting them off with scissors. Fry until golden brown, about 2-3 minutes. Drain on paper towels.
Coat with Sugar and Cinnamon:
Mix together 1/2 cup sugar and cinnamon. Roll drained churros in the cinnamon-sugar mixture.

Serve:
Serve warm with chocolate sauce, caramel sauce, or dulce de leche for dipping. Enjoy your homemade churros! They're crispy on the outside, soft and fluffy on the inside, and perfectly coated in cinnamon sugar. Perfect for a sweet treat or dessert!

OREO CHEESECAKE

Ingredients:

24 Oreo cookies
5 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
32 ounces (900g) cream cheese, softened
1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
4 large eggs
1 cup (240ml) sour cream
1 cup (240ml) heavy cream
1/4 cup (30g) all-purpose flour
12 Oreo cookies, chopped
Instructions:

Preheat Oven:
Preheat your oven to 325°F (160°C). Wrap the outside of a 9-inch springform pan with aluminum foil. This prevents water from leaking into the pan during the water bath.
Crush Oreos:
Crush 24 Oreos in a food processor until they become fine crumbs. Alternatively, place them in a plastic bag and crush them with a rolling pin. Transfer the crumbs to a mixing bowl.
Mix Crumbs with Butter:
Add melted butter to the Oreo crumbs and mix until well combined. Press the mixture into the bottom of the prepared springform pan to form the crust.
Prepare Filling:
In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth using an electric mixer. Add granulated sugar and vanilla extract, and beat until creamy.
Add Eggs:
Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Scrape down the sides of the bowl as needed.
Add Sour Cream and Heavy Cream:
Mix in sour cream and heavy cream until smooth and creamy. Add flour and mix until just combined.
Fold in Chopped Oreos:
Gently fold in the chopped Oreos into the cheesecake batter.
Pour Batter into Pan:
Pour the cheesecake batter over the Oreo crust in the springform pan.
Bake:
Place the springform pan into a larger baking pan or roasting pan. Fill the larger pan with hot water halfway up the sides of the springform pan. This creates a water bath for even baking and prevents cracking. Bake in the preheated oven for 60-70 minutes, or until the edges are set but the center still jiggles slightly.
Cool and Chill:

Serve:
Before serving, carefully remove the cheesecake from the springform pan. Slice and enjoy!

SAMOSA

Ingredients:

2 large potatoes, boiled, peeled, and mashed
1/2 cup (75g) green peas, boiled
1 small onion, finely chopped
2 green chilies, finely chopped (adjust to taste)
1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
1 teaspoon cumin seeds
1 teaspoon coriander powder
1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
1/2 teaspoon garam masala
1/2 teaspoon amchur (dry mango powder)
Salt, to taste
Instructions:

Making the dough:
In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour and salt.
Add the vegetable oil or melted ghee and mix well until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs.
Gradually add water, a little at a time, and knead to form a smooth and firm dough. Cover the dough and let it rest for 30 minutes.
Making the filling:
Heat a tablespoon of oil in a pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent.
Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, and sauté for a minute. Add coriander powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, amchur, and salt. Mix well.
Add mashed potatoes and boiled green peas. Mix until well combined.
Cook the filling for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add chopped coriander leaves, mix well, and remove from heat. Allow the filling to cool completely.
Assembling and frying the samosas:
Divide the rested dough into small lemon-sized balls. Roll each ball into a smooth ball between your palms.
Roll out each dough ball into a thin oval or circular shape.
Cut the rolled dough in half to form two semi-circles. Take one semi-circle, brush water along the straight edge, and form a cone shape by overlapping the straight edges and pressing to seal.
Fill the cone with the prepared potato filling, leaving some space at the top. Brush water along the open edge and press to seal and secure the samosa.
Repeat the process with the remaining dough and filling to make more samosas.
Frying the samosas:
Heat vegetable oil in a deep frying pan or kadhai over medium heat. Once the oil is hot, gently slide in the prepared samosas, a few at a time, without overcrowding the pan.
Fry the samosas until they turn golden brown and crisp on all sides, turning occasionally. This may take about 8-10 minutes. Once fried, remove the samosas using a slotted spoon and drain excess oil on paper towels.
Serve hot with mint chutney, tamarind chutney, or ketchup.
Enjoy your homemade samosas as a delicious snack or appetizer!

BRIYANI

Ingredients:

500g chicken, cut into pieces
1 cup yogurt
2 cups basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes
2 onions, thinly sliced
2 tomatoes, chopped
2 green chilies, slit lengthwise
2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
Handful of mint leaves, chopped
Handful of coriander leaves, chopped
2 tablespoons biryani masala powder
Salt, to taste
Cooking oil or ghee
Whole spices (1 bay leaf, 2-3 green cardamom pods, 2-3 cloves, 1-inch cinnamon stick)
Saffron strands soaked in warm milk (optional)
Fried onions (optional)
Instructions:

Marinate chicken pieces with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt. Let it marinate for at least 1 hour. Cook soaked basmati rice with whole spices until 70-80% done. Drain and set aside.
In a pan, heat oil or ghee. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Sauté until fragrant.
Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft. Add biryani masala powder and salt. Mix well.
Add marinated chicken pieces along with any remaining marinade. Cook until chicken is partially cooked and masala thickens.
Layer cooked rice and chicken masala in a heavy-bottomed pot, alternating between them. Top with fried onions, mint leaves, and coriander leaves. Drizzle saffron-soaked milk over the top layer.
Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes (dum). Once done, let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
Serve hot with raita or salad. Enjoy your quick and delicious chicken biryani!

POORI

Ingredients:

1 cup whole wheat flour
Water, as needed for kneading the dough
Salt, to taste
Oil, for deep frying
Instructions:

Prepare the Dough:
In a mixing bowl, combine the whole wheat flour and salt. Gradually add water and knead until you form a smooth and soft dough. The dough should be firm but pliable.
Divide and Shape:
Divide the dough into small lemon-sized balls. Flatten each dough ball slightly between your palms.
Roll Out:
Heat the oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat. On a lightly floured surface, roll out each dough ball into a small circle, about 3-4 inches in diameter. Roll them thinner for crispy puris and slightly thicker for softer ones.
Fry the Puris:
Once the oil is hot, carefully slide in one rolled-out dough circle at a time. Gently press down with a slotted spoon as it puffs up. Fry until it turns golden brown on both sides.
Remove the puri using a slotted spoon and drain excess oil on paper towels.
Serve:

Serve the puris hot with your favorite curry, chole, or any other accompaniment of your choice. Enjoy your delicious small batch of puris!
